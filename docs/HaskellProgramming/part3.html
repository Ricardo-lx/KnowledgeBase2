Algebra generally refers to one of the most important fields of mathematics. In this usage, it means the study of mathe- matical symbols and the rules governing their manipulation. It is diÔ¨Äerentiated from arithmetic by its use of abstractions such as variables. By the use of variables, we‚Äôre saying we don‚Äôt care much what value will be put into that slot.
